Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I book a hotel on EZYSTAY?

Booking a hotel on EZYSTAY is easy! Simply visit our website or download our mobile app, enter your destination and travel dates, and browse through the available hotel options. Once you've found the perfect hotel, follow the prompts to complete your booking securely online.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. You can also choose to pay with PayPal or other third-party payment providers, depending on your preferences and availability.

3. Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

Yes, in most cases, you can modify or cancel your reservation through your EZYSTAY account. However, please note that the specific terms and conditions for modifications and cancellations may vary depending on the hotel and rate you've booked. We recommend reviewing the cancellation policy carefully before making your reservation.

4. Is my payment information secure?

Yes, EZYSTAY takes the security of your payment information seriously. We use industry-standard encryption technology to protect your data during transmission. Additionally, we adhere to strict security protocols and comply with all applicable regulations to ensure the safety and privacy of your payment information.

5. Do you offer customer support?

Absolutely! Our customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have. Whether you need help with booking a hotel, modifying a reservation, or resolving an issue during your stay, our dedicated support staff is here to help you every step of the way.

6. Can I earn rewards with EZYSTAY?

Yes, EZYSTAY offers a rewards program for frequent travelers. By signing up for our loyalty program, you can earn points for every booking you make, which can be redeemed for discounts, free nights, and other exclusive benefits. Joining is free, so start earning rewards on your next trip!
